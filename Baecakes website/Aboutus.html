About Us

Baecakes is a leading household cake bakery in Nairobi, Kenya. Our cakes are Fresh, Homemade and passionately baked to guarantee quality to our esteemed clients.

Launched in 2020, our brand has grown to be loved and opted by thousands within Nairobi environs. We take pride in providing the best quality cakes that not only offers satisfation but also Value for Money. By offering affordable rates, our customers are able to purchase cakes at their desired times. At the end everyone is Happy Be it We offer baking classes, cake deliveries and organise cake tasting and meetups. Our cake varieties range from White forest, Black forests, Queen cakes, Red Velvet,Cupcakes to weddings cakes. We have it all!
